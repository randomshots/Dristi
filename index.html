What is Dristi?

As a New age school, we educate our students to be technologically competent. As a proud student of Dristi Schools, we prepare our students to compete and collaborate on an National & international scale.

Transforming the Indian Schooling Experience

At Dristi, we are committed to transforming the school experience. From the moment you walk into a Dristi , you will notice the difference. Beyond the warm welcome and inviting environment is a team of highly trained teachers, administrators, and support personnel, all of whom share the goal of ensuring that your child is highly engaged in their academic life.
